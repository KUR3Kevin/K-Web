How to Play

Game Objective

Build wealth and achieve financial goals by managing your income, expenses, investments, and debt wisely.

Key Concepts

  • Net Income: Income minus expenses. This is what you have left to save or invest.
  • Compound Interest: Earning interest on your interest. Your savings grow exponentially over time!
  • Investing: Buying stocks to grow wealth. Higher risk = higher potential returns.
  • Debt Interest: Money you pay for borrowing. High-interest debt grows quickly!
  • Net Worth: Total assets (cash + savings + investments) minus total debt.

Tips for Success

  • Keep expenses lower than income to build savings
  • Build an emergency fund before investing heavily
  • Pay off high-interest debt (credit cards) first
  • Diversify investments across different stocks
  • Be patient - compound interest works best over time!
